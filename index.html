How I work

It's all about digging deep to truly understand our customers ‚Äì their needs, their everyday struggles, and what makes them tick. I then take those insights and turn them into clear guidance that helps our teams build products that are not just impactful, but genuinely user-centered.

I love leading user research projects from start to finish.
